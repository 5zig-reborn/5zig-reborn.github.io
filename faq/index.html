The 5zig Mod - FAQ

All-in-one modification for Minecraft 1.8.9-1.15.2.
Revamp the way you play the game!

What is this?

This is The 5zig Mod you all use and love, but with updates and added functionality after the original author, 5zig, ended support for it.

Are you 5zig?

No, we are a group of developers working on this as a hobby project.
If you wish to contact the original author, you can do so on Twitter.

Can I get my cape back?

No, or at least, not yet. If you had a cape on the old 5zig mod, you won't be able to get one for free on 5zig Reborn.

How do I get a cape then?

You can get a cape by donating. Currently, you can either get one by pledging through Patreon, or by logging onto the web panel using the "Site Login" feature.
You can find it in The 5zig Mod > Chat.

When will version x be supported?

When a new version gets released, we have to wait for Forge to support it.
Then, we need to make sure everything works on it. It is a very long process that could take even months.

Is the mod open source?

It is!
It is available on GitHub under the GPLv3 license.
